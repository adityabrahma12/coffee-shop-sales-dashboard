# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Excel Dashboard & Business Analysis project** built using retail sales data from a coffee shop business.

The goal of this project was not just to create charts and dashboards, but to think like a real business analyst and answer practical business questions that can help a coffee shop improve its sales, operations, and customer experience.

This project focuses on transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can support better decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The main objective of this project was to analyze coffee shop sales data and identify:

* customer buying patterns
* peak sales hours
* best-selling products
* store performance
* revenue trends
* customer spending behavior

The analysis was designed to generate actionable insights that could help improve:

* operational efficiency
* revenue growth
* inventory planning
* staffing decisions
* product strategy

---

# 📊 Key Business Questions Solved

This project answers the following business questions:

1. How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
2. Are there any peak times for sales activity?
3. What is the total sales revenue for each month?
4. How do sales vary across different store locations?
5. What is the average order value per customer?
6. Which products are the best-selling in terms of quantity and revenue?
7. How do sales vary by product category and product type?

Additional analysis was also performed to identify:

* opportunities for upselling
* operational optimization areas
* marketing focus areas

# 🔍 Key Insights

Some major insights discovered during the analysis:

* Morning hours generated the highest sales activity.
* Coffee products contributed the highest revenue.
* Revenue showed strong month-on-month growth.
* Store performance was relatively balanced across locations.
* Average order value indicated opportunities for upselling and combo offers.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were suggested:

* Increase staffing during peak morning hours
* Introduce combo offers to increase average order value
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories
* Improve upselling strategies
* Expand premium beverage offerings
